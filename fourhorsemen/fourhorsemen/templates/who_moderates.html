Who Moderates?

Less surprisingly, we found that most participants showed a preference for human moderation and judgment in determining whether content was genuinely harassing. We just don’t trust algorithms enough yet.

However, what about the lives of the humans who have to dig through this shit, day in and day out? If receiving this type of message is traumatic, is it also traumatic to have to comb through thousands of similar messages (many with images!) per hour? (Short answer: Yes.) Human moderators might do a better job, but is it worth the risks to their health? And they don’t scale as well as computers. If you want to learn more, you might want to check out some recent media coverage about content moderators:

  • The Verge: “THE SECRET RULES OF THE INTERNET: The murky history of moderation, and how it’s shaping the future of free speech”
